About RealDiag

RealDiag is a decision-support platform that integrates national and international medical guidelines with live diagnostic data from electronic health records. Our mission is to reduce variability in referral decisions, prevent missed opportunities for specialist care, and accelerate patient access to life-saving treatments.

Key Features

Guideline Integration

Centralized access to AAN, ADA, NICE, and other medical guidelines, updated in real-time.

Diagnostic Intelligence

Connects patient history, labs, and imaging with referral rules for precise recommendations.

EHR Integration

FHIR/HL7-ready APIs that embed directly into Epic, Cerner, and other EHR systems.
